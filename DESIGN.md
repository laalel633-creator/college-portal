# Design Brief

## Direction

Casual, Approachable School — friendly college website without corporate polish. Plain fonts, flat colors, zero decoration.

## Tone

Simple, trustworthy, unpretentious. Like a real community college site from 2010. No pretense, just straightforward and easy.

## Differentiation

All-sans-serif typography (no serif display), flat blue + white + gray palette. Functional clarity over sophistication.

## Color Palette

| Token          | OKLCH       | Role                    |
|----------------|-------------|-------------------------|
| background     | 0.99 0.001 0 | Off-white, neutral base |
| foreground     | 0.2 0.01 0  | Dark gray text          |
| card           | 1.0 0 0     | Pure white sections     |
| primary        | 0.5 0.1 240 | Friendly blue — CTAs    |
| muted          | 0.93 0.005 0| Light gray dividers     |
| destructive    | 0.55 0.2 25 | Red for errors          |

## Typography

- Display: Figtree — friendly sans-serif for all headings
- Body: DM Sans — clear, readable body text
- Mono: JetBrains Mono — form inputs and code
- Scale: hero `text-4xl`, h2 `text-2xl`, body `text-base`

## Elevation & Depth

Zero decoration: solid colors, simple 1px borders, no shadows. Depth from color only.

## Structural Zones

| Zone    | Background          | Border       | Notes           |
|---------|---------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Header  | card (white)        | border-b     | Plain nav       |
| Hero    | background (off-white) | —          | Welcome text    |
| Content | card (white)        | border       | Simple sections |
| Footer  | muted (light gray)  | border-t     | Contact info    |

## Spacing & Rhythm

Simple rhythm: 2rem section gaps, 1rem internal padding, 0.5rem micro. Breathing room.

## Component Patterns

- Buttons: Blue with white text, no hover effects
- Cards: White bg, 1px border, no shadow
- Forms: White backgrounds, gray borders
- Navigation: Plain text links, no animations

## Motion

Minimal — no entrance animations, no hover transitions. Static and straightforward.

## Constraints

- No gradients, no shadows, no decorative effects
- Flat colors only (white, gray, blue)
- Minimal borders (1px only)
- All sans-serif typography
- No animations or transitions

## Signature Detail

Pure functional simplicity — a college website that says "we're here to help you learn" without flashy design.
